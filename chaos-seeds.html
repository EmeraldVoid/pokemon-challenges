Introduction

Chaos Seeds are special seeds created by Emerald_Void that randomize everything—items, moves, abilities, and more. If it can be randomized, it will be. This introduces an unpredictable and chaotic challenge into an already difficult gameplay experience.

Chaos Seeds are designed to be fun, goofy, and extremely difficult. Any Pokémon that survives this grueling challenge earns a well-deserved place in Emerald's Hall of Fame.

Rules

The specific rules for Chaos Seeds depend on the format of the night. Chaos Seeds may follow either Kaizo Ironmon rules or Nuzlocke rules.

When a viewer redeems a Chaos Seed, Emerald_Void will load a randomly pre-generated Chaos Seed and begin playing.

General Rules

  • All healing moves are banned
  • Wonder Guard is banned

BST Restrictions

BST restrictions depend on:

  • The game being played
  • The format being used (Ironmon vs. Egglocke)

Catching Pokémon

Kaizo Ironmon Rules

  • You may only use one Pokémon at all times.
  • The only additional Pokémon allowed are HM Friends.
  • If you wish to pivot, you must commit to the new Pokémon upon catching it, before checking its stats.

Nuzlocke Rules

  • You may catch the first Pokémon encountered in each route/location.
  • If the Pokémon is knocked out, you cannot catch another Pokémon in that area.

Notes

  • ¹ Rules may change over time. If updates occur, old rules will be archived for those who want to attempt past versions.
  • ² Occasionally, Chaos Seeds may be generated live on stream instead of being pre-generated.
